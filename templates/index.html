ü§î Common Questions:
Q: Is it bad if I have open ports?
A: Not necessarily! Many devices need open ports to function. The concern is which ports and who can access them.
Q: Can hackers access my network through these ports?
A: Only if the ports are exposed to the internet AND have vulnerabilities. Behind a router, you're usually safe.
Q: Should I close all open ports?
A: No! Close only unused/unfamiliar ones. Your router needs ports 53/80/443 to work.
Q: I found a device I don't recognize!
A: Could be a neighbor's device, a guest device, or something with a generic name. Check your router's device list.
